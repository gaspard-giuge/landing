LEARN ABOUT SPORT

Modern sport is a British invention, imported into Switzerland via tourism and private schools. Sport comes from "desport", an old French word meaning "to amuse oneself". It was invented in England during the industrial revolution in the 19th century. In France and until 1900, the 'desport' makes a difficult beginning: the pioneers of the French sport are few and are confronted with the lack of interest of the public authorities but the law of 1901 allows then the sport to be organized in clubs During the first world war, the soldiers were occupied by activities such as soccer, rugby, boxing developed at the back of the front Then, a national craze for sport emerges from 1919 and becomes a real phenomenon of society.
